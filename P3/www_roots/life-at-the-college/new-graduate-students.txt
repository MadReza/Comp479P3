http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/offices/sgs/new-graduate-students.htmlWelcome and Congratulations! Starting your new life as a Concordia student is an exciting time. Here's how to get oriented, connect with your fellow graduate students and make a smooth transition to life on campus.  Meet with your Graduate Program Director (GPD) or Assistant (GPA) for any academic matters (including course selection). They are responsible for managing all aspects of your program and for advising students on academic matters and, for some programs, course registration.
  Find out what it takes to be a successful graduate student.
