Alexandra Dawson joined the John Molson School of Business in 2009. She has a PhD in Management and Business Administration from Bocconi University and an MSc Econ from the London School of Economics. 
Dr Dawson's research focuses on entrepreneurial and family businesses. She received a Dean's Award for Distinguished Scholarship in 2014.
As the Director of the National Bank Initiative in Entrepreneurship and Family Business, Dr Dawson is responsible for overseeing research and supporting students in the areas of entrepreneurship and family business. http://www.concordia.ca/nationalbank
As the Director of the Concordia Small Business Consulting Bureau, Dawson oversees a group of five MBA students offering business consulting services to entrepreneurs and organizations in the Greater Montreal area. http://www.concordia.ca/csbcb

Prior to academia, Dawson was a manager with a strategy consulting firm in London, UK. In that role, she provided strategy advice to corporate clients and investment advice to private equity firms, helping them evaluate the commercial viability of potential investments.
