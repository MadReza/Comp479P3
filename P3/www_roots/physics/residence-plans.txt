Once your student ID card is activated with your Dining Dollars Plan, your Dining Dollars are used like cash at the Food Service Outlets.You can choose to add more Dining Dollars anytime during the year by simply loading a preferred amount (minimum $25) to your Dining Dollars Plan.

a coffee (using your reusable mug only)
