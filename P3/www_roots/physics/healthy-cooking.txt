Participants cook 5 different recipes to take home.How it works·        A fun, hands-on cooking class meant to equip participants with nutritious, simple and affordable meal ideas and evidenced-based nutrition recommendations. These sessions offer practical tips on how to add variety to meal planning and provide participants with delicious recipes, modified by a dietitian.Healthy cooking classes are offered on demand, call to schedule your group. A list of possible classes are available are described below, however tailored menus are also available.Roll out your sushi skills with special guest, Chef Daniel, and learn to make small changes one meal at a time.
If you ever wondered about the difference between saturated and unsaturated fats and where to find omega-3’s, then this session is for you! A special look at popular fats such as olive oil, coconut fat, and cooking for diabetes and heart-health.
Have you recently been diagnosed with a gastro-intestinal condition and a specialized diet? Getting enough daily fiber for optimal health, while minimizing complications is often a concern. Are words like "fermentable carbohydrates", "soluble and insoluble fiber" piquing your interest? Do you wonder if you are choosing foods that give you enough fibre? Learn how to incorporate fibre rich foods into your diet, the Mediterranean way!How can you spin a simple recipe in several different savory directions while saving money and reducing salt or sugar? This session focuses on spices, seasonings and low-processed food group combinations. A special look at cooking for diabetes, heart health and gastrointestinal conditions.Whether you are vegetarian, pescatarian, or you eat meat occasionally, if you are interested in sustainable food habits, saving money and eating well, then this session is for you!
