http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/events/offices/provost/ssc/2016/10/7/New-Student-Tour-Places-to-Eat-SGW.htmlNot sure where to get a meal that won’t break your piggy bank? Then you need to take this tour! It’s a great opportunity to discover healthy, tasty and affordable food options around the SGW campus, and your tour guides will be Student Success Mentors.We will also share some tips on where to grocery shop on the budget and talk about some cool places to eat when you have friends (or parents!) visit Montreal.This is a great opportunity to meet new friends as we will enjoy a group dinner at the end of the tour.© Concordia UniversityConcordia University
