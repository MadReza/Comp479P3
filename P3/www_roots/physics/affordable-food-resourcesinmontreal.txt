The People’s Potato offers vegan lunches on a by-donation basis.  They serve meals from 12:30-2pm on the 7th floor of the Hall Building on weekdays during the fall and winter semesters.  They have a Montreal Food Assistance Guide with a food assistance list.  From their website you can download two vegan cookbooks for free (Vegan on a Shoestring #1, Vegan on a Shoestring #2).
They also have a foodbank, opportunities to volunteer and workshops.Hive Free Lunch at Loyola
The Concordia Student Union provides a free vegetarian lunch every weekday during the during the fall and winter semesters from 12:30-1:30 pm.  Located in the Hive (2nd floor of SC building).

Mother Hubbard’s Cupboard
Multi-faith and Spirituality Centre offers a vegan supper every Thursday from 5 pm - 7 pm during the fall and winter semesters.  A two dollar (2$) donation is requested.  Located at 2090 Mackay Street room Z105/106 (514-848-2424 extension 3593 or mfsc@concordia.ca).

Emergency Food Fund
Multi-faith and Spirituality Centre provides vouchers to local grocery stores.  Call 514-848-2424 extension 3593 or e-mail mfsc@concordia.ca to make an appointment.

Le FrigoVert
This is a non-profit food cooperative on campus, located at 1440 Mackay.The Information and Referral Center of Greater Montreal
This organization directs people seeking help to hundreds of community resources that provide the services they need.  They can help you find food banks and a lot more in your neighbourhood.  Call 514-527-1375.arrondissement.com
This website provides information on the services available in the various Montreal districts.  Select your area from the drop down menu at the upper left (default is "Tout Arrondissement.com") and then select "alimentation" from the "Catégories" dropdown menu (again in the upper left) to discover the many food related services in your district.The Multi-faith and Spirituality Centre lists many off-campus food resources on their website.

Second Life
Offers the opportunity to buy 'ugly produce' - fruits and vegetables that are misshapen or undersized, but otherwise perfectly good - at discount prices.
