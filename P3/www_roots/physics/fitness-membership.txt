http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/research/perform/community-programs/fitness-membership.htmlHow is PERFORM different from your normal gym? A fitness membership in the PERFORM Gym allows members to benefit from the very best. They have access to the latest teaching methods, the most recent research findings, and to the most advanced fitness equipment which allows them to track their personal goals and their progress. Members who are looking for additional support while exercising can also benefit from PERFORM's experts in the field of exercise and preventative health and the various health programs and services that they have developed. Members are encouraged to take part in PERFORM's research and training programs.Register at the PERFORM Centre reception desk anytime between 6:30 a.m. and 10 p.m., Monday through Friday, and 8 a.m. and 6 p.m., Saturday and Sunday.Tailor your membership to your needs; various prices are listed to the right.Payment can be made by Interac, VISA, Mastercard or American Express.Need help getting started on the right track? Check out our Personal Trainers. The PERFORM Gym operates according to the University holiday calendar.Rates shown are applicable as of Fall 2016 semester, starting September 6, 2016The listed rates are before any applicable taxes.
