This exciting program provides Concordia students the opportunity to participate in an educational and cultural immersion at the Universidad Pontificia de Salamanca while earning 12 Concordia credits in Spanish language courses.In collaboration with Concordia International, the Faculty of Fine Arts and the Department of Theatre are pleased to offer Concordia Theatre students the exceptional opportunity to participate in a summer exchange program in Erlangen, Germany.
