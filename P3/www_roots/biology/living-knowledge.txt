http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/about/community/initiatives/living-knowledge.htmlThe Living Knowledge community of practice brings together faculty and staff who share a passion for community engaged scholarship, with the goal of creating connections, sharing ideas and strengthening our collective practice. Students, community organizers and representatives from other universities are also welcome.During the 2016-2017 academic year, we will host four lunch time events, each centered around a particular question or theme. Check out the details below and Save The Date! For more information, or to RSVP, please email livingknowledge@concordia.ca.
