http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/success/about-ssc.htmlFor Concordia students, the Student Success Centre is key to bridging the academic experience with individual and professional development. We assist you at every stage of your journey to becoming an academically-skilled and career-prepared graduate, equipped to meet future challenges and to be a life-long learner.Bringing together a variety of services that once fell under the banner of Counselling and Development, the Centre draws together a support network to aid you in finding the resources and opportunities to enhance your program of study and realize and achieve your goals.Our professional staff and student team can help you to:We also offer multiple outreach events over the course of the academic year, including one-day time management clinics at the start of each term where students can create an individual schedule with one of our specialists, and Student Success Centre ‘On the Road’ pop-up information tables to provide tips and encouragement during exam time.What’s next? We’re currently in the design stages of a new space in the downtown campus so we can reach more students with one-stop, made-to-measure support. We’re also designing exciting new programs and opportunities. Stay tuned!Through a series of ‘visioning’ sessions with our staff and students, the Student Success Centre team developed a mission, vision and values statement of our purpose and aims.We are a welcoming community working collaboratively to engage and empower every student to achieve individual, academic and professional success.Prepared for today, ready for tomorrow.© Concordia UniversityLearning support

                Successfully transition into university life with the mentorship of a successful, upper-year Concordia student and orientation events that promote a rewarding first-year experience.Develop your academic skills with one-on-one support from a Learning Specialist, aided by our peer tutors in writing, math and science who run customized learning sessions, study groups and exam preparation for core pre-requisite and commonly difficult courses. Our English and French language conversation groups are also a great way to practice and improve as well as to meet other students.Plan your career and find employment with our CAPS team, who assist students in finding opportunities to develop specific job or career-related skills. In addition to individual appointments, you can find workshops, an online job bank, employer fairs, information sessions and on-campus interviews and career panels.Be recognised for your success through awards and scholarships. The Centre supports Concordia applicants for the prestigious Rhodes Scholarship, and will add an expanded profile of application assistance for competitive student awards in the coming years.Empowering student growth and development by building skills through individualized appointments, workshops and print/online materials, and by mentoring and encouraging students through peer and professional support (including listening, validating and coaching).Demonstrating respect by understanding and responding to needs, and acknowledging diversity by sensitively taking this into account.Making connections by providing resources and fostering relationships through listening and outreach.Making a difference by being aware of and responsive to the Concordia community by providing guidance, resources and expertise.Collaborating with students, faculty, staff, alumni, industry professionals, employers, and other universities, through programming, services and events to achieve student success, and across units to create joint projects to reflect our mission and vision.Student Success CentreAbout the Student Success CentreLearning supportLearning specialistsHandouts for learning & writingCouse-specific helpPeer tutorsWriting assistanceTalk n learn for ESL studentsStrategic Learning ProgramWorkshopsNew studentsDiscover ConcordiaGraduate Experience SeminarsGraduate student orientationMAP to successNew student toursStart rightWorkshopsGuide to First YearCareer and Planning ServicesAlumni servicesCareer counsellingCareer events and job fairsCareer resource centreCareer workshopsEmployersEmployeursInterview skillsJob SearchJob & internship searchOnline job bankResearching salaries & industriesRésumés & cover lettersWhat can I do with my major?Student success mentorsRequest a mentorHiring MentorsRhodes Scholarshipsfacebooktwittergoogleyoutubelinkedinpinterestinstagramflickr© Concordia UniversityTermsPrivacyAccessibilityWebsite feedback
