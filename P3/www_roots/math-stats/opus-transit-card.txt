http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/birks/opus-transit-card.htmlTo be entitled to pay reduced fare (a 40% savings off the regular fare), you must obtain a registered OPUS photo ID card. You can order your card online and it will be sent to you by mail. It’s fast and easy, with no waiting in line!Before requesting a card online, be sure you meet these criteria:You will be charged a $15 fee to issue a card.Login to MyConcordia portal using your netname and password.You will need proof of age and $15 cash to cover the cost of the card.
