http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/birks/welcome-centre.htmlIf you’re in the neighborhood and want to learn more about Concordia, stop in and say hello!The Welcome Centre is a great starting point for prospective students, family members, and visitors. At the Welcome Centre, you can:We set up campus tours all year round. Ask your parents or friends to join you — everyone’s welcome to come along.Book a tour online by creating a profile on Connect2Concordia. Or simply contact us.Are you a school counsellor? We can help you set up a group tour.An Admissions Information Officer would be happy to discuss general admissions information and answer your questions. To book an appointment, call us (514-848-2424 ext. 2668) or drop by.
