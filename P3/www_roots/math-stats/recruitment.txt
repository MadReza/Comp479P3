http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/admissions/recruitment.htmlThere are many ways to meet us, both on- and off-campus, in Montreal or in your city. Campus tours, Open House, information sessions... Our doors are always open.The Welcome Centre is a great starting point for prospective students, family members, and visitors. Drop by the Welcome Centre to:We set up campus tours all year round. Student guides will lead you around campus (SGW, Loyola or both) and highlight student services or specific facilities you’d like to see. So book a personal tour and bring your friends.School counsellors: We can prepare a customized tour for your students.CREDIT: Warren Zelman PhotographySave the date! Open House and Portfolio Day (for prospective Fine Arts students) are held in the fall. We also invite newly-admitted students to attend our information sessions that cover the next steps for soon-to-be Concordia students.
  Our recruitment team attends high schools and education fairs across Canada and around the world.  If you can’t make it to campus in person, check our travel schedule to see when we plan to visit your area.Reach out to one of our recruiters to ask a question about our programs, admissions process or student life in Montreal.
