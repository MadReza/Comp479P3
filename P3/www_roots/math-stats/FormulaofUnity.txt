http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/artsci/math-stats/about/math-outreach/FormulaofUnity.htmlThe International Mathematical Olympiad “Formula of Unity” / “The Third Millennium” is organized by the St. Petersburg State University and the Euler Foundation, and it was first held in the 2013-2014 academic year. During its 2014-2015 edition, it gathered more than 6,000 students from 15 countries.In Montreal, the participation in the “Formula of Unity” is facilitated by the Department of Mathematics and Statistics of Concordia University and l'Institut des sciences mathématiques (ISM). - The first round is being held from October 20 to November 18, 2016 by correspondence.The First Round, October 20—November 18, 2016, is being held by correspondence. Students from grades 6—12 are invited.Problems for the 1st (Fall) round are open. Please consult the webpage for the problems:
 http://www.formulo.org/en/olimp/ol17/In order to know in which category you should enroll, please consult the file about matching grades:
 http://malnova.formulo.org/media/cms_page_media/221/Table%20of%20matching%20grades_NLhBDCd.pdfInstructions about submitting work are specified on the page:1.     You have the option to submit your solutions online, directly to the central organization. Please, read
        instructions on the page:  http://www.formulo.org/en/olimp/ol17/           2.     Or, you can forward your solutions to montreamathclub@gmail.com, before November 18. Please,
        specify the information requested by organizers.  Check the webpage for more info:
        http://www.formulo.org/en/olimp/ol17/
