We consider your eligibility based on your age, experience and potential. The MEP requires the successful completion of a minimum of 18 additional credits.In order to apply to the Mature Entry Program, you must meet the following conditions:If you do not qualify as a Mature Student, you may want to investigate the possibility of becoming an Independent Student.
