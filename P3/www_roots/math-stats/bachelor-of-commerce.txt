http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/academics/degrees/bachelor-of-commerce.htmlThe John Molson School of Business offers BComm degrees that provide you with an education for many careers in business. You may choose any of these fields of study for your undergraduate degree:  You will have the opportunity to take part in co-operative education programs, available in the following programs: Accountancy, Business Technology Management, Finance, Human Resource Management, International Business, Marketing, and Supply Chain Operations Management.You must select a major in the BComm program. You may also complete an optional 12 credit Business minor in addition to your major, however, you may not take both a major and minor in the same discipline. The 90 credit BComm program consists of the following:Find a complete program overview on the JMSB websiteEveryone entering a John Molson School of Business degree program must complete a common set of 14 required core courses.If you graduated from a secondary school outside Quebec, then the Extended Credit Program (ECP) is designed for you.
