http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/research/d3center.htmlDistrict 3 is a community dedicated to collaboration, innovation and entrepreneurship. Located in a vibrant work space, Concordia’s students, faculty, and alumni collaborate across disciplines on challenging, real world initiatives, transforming bold ideas into a positive impact for society.The Centre's goals are to:Creativity: District 3 is a place to gain invaluable experience in the idea-to-prototype process through collaborating with other emerging experts in the ecosystem.Innovation Incubator: Concordia alumni and entrepreneurs from small to medium enterprises come to District 3 with mandates for innovation projects. District 3 creates multidisciplinary teams to move from idea to impact.Startup Incubator: Entrepreneurs in the making with innovative ideas can meet with our experts in residence who will coach and mentor them through market validation, product development, and pitching to potential stakeholders. Every step of the way, networking opportunities help point the way to success (or early, productive failure!) and provide invaluable practical learning.A Knowledge Space: Dedicated Concordia faculty members offer cutting-edge theoretical and practical research in best practices of innovation and entrepreneurship, providing a set of tools and methods to effectively “think outside the box.”A Social Space: Concordia community members interested in innovation and entrepreneurship meet and learn about activities and initiatives in the District 3 ecosystem – or just enjoy a cup of coffee in a buzzing, vital atmosphere.
 District 3 meets students’ needs for practical experience, vital to securing employment after graduation. With District 3’s expert guidance, determination and hard work, an entrepreneurial idea might even lead to a breakthrough start-up business!For alumni and leaders, Concordia offers valuable resources to transform ideas into material impact for our society. Prototyping and user-validation have never been cheaper, faster, or easier. With a skilled multidisciplinary team working hard toward a common goal, there is practically no limit to what can be achieved.For Quebec and Canada, there is no doubt: innovation and entrepreneurship are the key to ongoing prosperity and growth.
