http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/jmsb/programs/co-op/btm.htmlBusiness Technology Management (BTM) is the use of information technology to improve organizational performance and individual quality of work life. The BTM Co-op program puts emphasiso on teaching the student the ability to identify business tasks and their information needs. It also provides project management, interpersonal communication skills, and a solid knowledge of information technology that is necessary to propose effective information systems solutions to given business problems.
