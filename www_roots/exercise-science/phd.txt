Students in the joint program also have access to the affiliated universities’ library systems, computer facilities, and presentations by internationally recognized researchers.A major gift from Montreal-based National Bank offers a $20,000 fellowship to JMSB PhD in Administration students with research interests in entrepreneurship and family business. Approved research projects may be selected from any of the PhD specializations.
