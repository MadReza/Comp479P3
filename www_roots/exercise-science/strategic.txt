If you want to know the best way to handle your reading load, written assignments, problem-solving courses, oral presentations or any other type of academic task, make an appointment with one of our Learning Specialists. We are all teachers, so we know what teachers expect, and we know Concordia courses. We can help graduate students as well as undergraduates in any faculty.To make an appointment with one of the Learning Specialists, drop by one of our offices or call us.
