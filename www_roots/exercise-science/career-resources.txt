http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/success/career-planning-services/career-resources.htmlThe Career Resource Centre or CRC, is located on the SGW Campus in room H-440. The drop-in centre provides print, electronic and audio-visual resources to help students in their career exploration. We also have an extensive collection of leadership, educational planning, career planning and job search resources. Our dedicated staff will help you find the information you need. They collect a wide range of materials to help you achieve your utmost when it comes to your personal growth and development. In addition, computer workstations in the Career Resource Centre are available to students for accessing the Internet. We also have a study space accessible for students. All students are welcome!A collection of career books and DVDs available at the Career Resource Centre.​ Check out our new Digital Library to find career & life success material that can be viewed online through the Concordia Library. These resources are available to current students and registered alumni. See what's in the digital library
