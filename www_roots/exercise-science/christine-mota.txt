                    A graduate of Concordia University, Christine Mota has worked in communications at Concordia for more than 20 years. Before that, she had a 15-year career in the Montreal broadcast industry working at various radio and TV stations as a newscaster, producer and talk-show host.In January 2006, she was appointed Director of Media Relations and official university spokesperson. Her primary responsibilities include setting communications priorities, providing strategic counsel and support to the senior administration and representing the university in the media.
