http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/events/artsci/sdbi/2015/03/13/sdbi-celebrates-40-years-giv.htmlTo celebrate the 40th anniversary of Groupe Intervention Vidéo (GIV), the Simone de Beauvoir Institute (SdBI) underlines its favourite films and videos from the extensive GIV collection, which offers a wealth of documentary, animation, and experimental works that explore women's realities and present a critical analysis of gendered injustice. Following the screening, there will be a reception in the atrium of the Library Building, adjacent to the Québec on the Move exhibition.This event is part of Quebec on the Move, an art exhibit and event series about social movements in Quebec.
