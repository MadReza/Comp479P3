http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/jmsb/about/awards/distinction.htmlThe Awards of Distinction is an initiative that was put forward by the late Honorable Pierre Sévigny P.C., O.C., in 1987, to publicly honour business excellence. It has since evolved into an annual awards ceremony that is regularly attended by hundreds of guests from the business and university community.The Awards Committee comprises representatives from JMSB Administration, three full-time Faculty members, one undergraduate student, one graduate student and one alumni representative.
