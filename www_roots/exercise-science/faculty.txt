Each department page lists the full-time members of the department and links to their profiles, which describe their current and previous research interests, publications and provides their contact information.

Depending on a student's chosen path of study you will learn from our top academic researchers who enrich the curriculum with their knowledge and experience.

In total, there are 134 full-time faculty from 26 countries, who are able to provide a global perspective to your professional business education.
