                    Marisa Lancione joined the Media Relations department in September 2012. She holds a Bachelor of Arts and Master of Arts in English Literature from Brock University. Marisa is a passionate mental health advocate and has volunteered as a social media coordinator for Bell Let’s Talk and Clara’s Big Ride and other mental health events in Montreal.As Media Relations coordinator, she provides targeted support for the entire Media Relations team.
