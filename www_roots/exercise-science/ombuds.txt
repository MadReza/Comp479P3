The office is:You can give us a call, send us an email or visit our office from Monday to Friday between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. Evening appointments as well as appointments on the Loyola campus can be made upon request. Our services are available in English and in French and are free of charge. We are always pleased to answer questions.
