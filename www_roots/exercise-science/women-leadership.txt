Many are leaders in their field, each with her own networks and experiences to share.The Concordia Alumni Women and Leadership program connects our alumnae, both in person and online, facilitating the exchange of ideas, information and experiences — to inspire and learn from each other.Prominent Concordia alumnae present on topics of personal and professional significance to established and aspiring leaders.Connect with our Women and Leadership group on LinkedIn. Post articles, contribute to discussions, ask questions, refer business, share stories, tips and guidance with fellow alumnae around the world.Sign up as an experienced professional to mentor other women. If you're a new graduate or mid-career and looking for advice, tap into the wisdom and experience of others.View members of the Concordia Alumni Women and Leadership advisory council and program champions.
