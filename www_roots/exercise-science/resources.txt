International students are aided in their matriculation process by the International Students' Office, which advises students on Canadian and Québec immigration processes.

Financial Aid and Awards
The Financial Aid and Awards Office (FAAO) understands the importance and the intricacies of financing your education. Their knowledgeable and dedicated staff is committed to providing each student with personalized in-depth assistance with their student aid requirements. Assistantships are awarded by the Department of Creative Arts Therapies each year, and are allocated for the Fall term. 

Counselling & Development
The mission of Counselling & Development is to empower students to maximize their personal, academic, and career potential. They provide diverse programs, resources and expertise in a supportive student-centred environment to facilitate student success.Student Life
