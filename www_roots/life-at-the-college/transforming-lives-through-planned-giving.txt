Hall, principal of Sir George Williams University (1957 to 1962), who oversaw one of Concordia’s two founding institutions.Before that encounter, Mikalachki didn’t think she’d achieve her dream of attending university — a far-off goal given her family’s difficult financial circumstances. Thanks to a scholarship granted by Hall, Mikalachki gained entrance to university. She made the most of every Georgian moment, developed a strong appreciation for learning and even met her future husband, Al Mikalachki, BA 58.Dorothy's generosity ensures the next generation of students can enjoy the same opportunities she had.Dorothy Mikalachki has honoured her alma mater by making a planned gift.
