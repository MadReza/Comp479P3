Enjoy a traditional holiday meal with all the trimmings.

Hospitality Concordia welcomes everyone to this popular event! Don’t miss this opportunity to mingle outside of the office or classroom while enjoying a traditional holiday feast in the company of your friends and co-workers.

Don’t forget to reserve in advance for take-out or eat-in.

When: Thursday, December 8th, 12:00 p.m. to 2:00 p.m.
Where: Engineering, Computer Science and Visual Arts Integrated Complex (EV), SGW Campus

RSVP Online for SGW Campus by December 2.

The cost is $12.00 per person (taxes included) for a special holiday themed menu.

Reserve online for individual or group seatings and for all take-out orders.
Payment can be made in cash on-site upon entry.
