http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/jmsb/executive-education/programs/aviation/avsec-management-certificate.htmlOverview    Program structure    Admission requirements    Dates & fee    Syllabus    ContactAviation security has become a top priority for the 191 member States of the International Civil Aviation Organization. Aviation security is a global responsibility. It is now international and it requires the participation of all stakeholders.Aviation security needs management personnel with the skills to work in multi-cultural teams and environments. The Aviation Security (AVSEC) Professional Management Certificate (PMC) is the first and only one of its kind in the world. This certificate was created in partnership with the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO), a specialized agency of the United Nations.AVSEC-PMC is aligned to ICAO’s strategic objectives and contributes to the Next Generation of Aviation Professionals (NGAP) initiative.The AVSEC-PM is a 12 week program (2 weeks on site and 10 weeks online).The curriculum of this course is comprised of 70% security and 30% management. The main objective of the AVSEC-PMC is to promote the use of management principles and best practices relevant to the field of AVSEC by applying training strategies including e-learning.Ideal candidates include:This program is compatible with a full-time job, but it requires an average of 15 to 20 hours per week of work. This includes:A high-speed internet connection is highly recommended although the system can function at lower speed.The admission requirements for the certificate are:Due to the heavy workload, including reading material, assignments and the team presentation, strong English skills are a must.Dates and locations:
 February 6 - April 28, 2017 – Toulouse – Delivered in French
 August - November, 2017 – Middle East
 September - December, 2017 – Asia PacificLanguage: English
 Cost: $5,400 CAD 
 This does not include the costs for travel and accommodations for the two in-person weeks. Cancellation policy
The certificate participants will be provided with guidelines for a common understanding of ICAO's Annex 17 Standards and Recommended Practices and the Security Manual.Contact us if you wish to receive more information about the AVSEC Professional Management Certificate.Aliya Ahmed
