http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/news/main/stories/2016/02/04/wanted--outstanding-concordians.htmlThe search has begun for outstanding professors, staff and students who go beyond expectations to enrich university and community life!
These three awards are a great way to recognize someone for making a difference. Nominate someone by February 19. It's easy and rewarding.
 The Concordia Council on Student Life is calling for nominations of individuals who have made extraordinary contributions to student life at the university: a professor who mentors students outside of the classroom; a staff member who exceeds the expectations of his or her job; a student whose involvement beyond the classroom enriches campus life  find out more and complete a nomination form by February 19.

Each year, the LIVE Centre presents these awards to Concordia students, staff and faculty who have made a significant contribution as volunteers, sharing their time and talent for the benefit of the Concordia, Montreal or the global community.See the guidelines for nominations and complete the nomination forms online. Presented annually, these awards are made possible by the Office of Community Engagement and the Office of the Provost to recognize one student, one faculty member and one community partner for their exemplary participation in community-engaged scholarship.Nominate someone today! Reminder: The deadline for all nominations is February 19, 2016, at 5 p.m.
