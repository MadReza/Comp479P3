http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/academics/undergraduate/calendar/current/pubinfo.htmlconcordia.caUnder the terms of an agreement between Concordia University and the provincial Ministère de l’Éducation, de l’Enseignement supérieur et de la Recherche, approved by the Quebec Access to Information Commission, Concordia University is required to transmit to the Ministry some or all of the following information concerning its students: the student’s permanent code, complete name, date of birth, gender, father’s complete name, mother’s complete name and place of birth. This information is being transmitted, at the Ministry’s request, in order to provide the Ministry with the tools to properly calculate funding for Concordia University, for planning purposes and to ensure the proper management of public funds.Editor: Cynthia Foster
