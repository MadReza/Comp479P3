The opportunity to study abroad is about much more than just travelling internationally and making new friends. It’s a way to add depth to your academic career by learning and researching at a new institution with different professors, assignments and expectations.Whether you choose an internship, an exchange or a field school, supplementing your studies with a global perspective is surprisingly accessible.From November 7 to 11, Concordia International invites you to discover global opportunities, meet exchange students from around the world and find out what living abroad is really like. With info sessions, a video viewing party and a day-long fair for Concordians to connect with students from all over, the Concordia International team is ready and available to answer all of your questions.For an immediate taste of the international experience, check out the top videos from the 2016 Study Abroad Video Contest:Ana Patricia Bourgeois, University of Madrid, Spain
