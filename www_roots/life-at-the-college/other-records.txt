Course descriptions for the current academic year and the previous years can be found in our Undergraduate Calendar website.We can provide general undergraduate course descriptions for earlier years at a cost of $5.00 per academic year. Please contact us for information on how to obtain older course descriptions.To obtain Graduate course descriptions please contact the appropriate graduate department.A record is a comprehensive report of a student's academic history at the University. It is a complete academic record and includes all courses followed at Concordia. If only a student record (unofficial transcript) is required, one can be obtained at the Birks Student Service Centre or by faxing a signed request to the Office of the Registrar at 514-848-2621.
