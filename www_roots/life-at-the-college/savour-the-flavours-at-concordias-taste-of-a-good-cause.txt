Don’t miss the fourth annual Taste of a Good Cause — a fun way to meet and learn about popular Concordia-approved caterers, enjoy some of their cooking and contribute funds to those who need it. Hosted by Hospitality Concordia, the October 27 event raises money for the Student Emergency and Food Fund, a service that offers grocery store gift cards to those requiring immediate financial assistance.  Enjoy cuisine beautifully and enthusiastically presented by caterers who donate all the food and their valuable time to help make this event a success.Planning an event at the university this year? Come get inspired as some of the featured caterers showcase menu samples geared towards student initiatives.Taste of a Good Cause is open to the entire Concordia community. Tasting samples can be purchased at $5 for three items or $10 for seven items. Please note that payment can be made in cash only on site.We look forward to seeing you there!
The fourth annual Taste of a Good Cause event takes place Thursday, October 27 from 12 to 2 p.m. in the atrium of the Engineering, Computer Science and Visual Arts Integrated Complex (EV Building), located at 1515 Rue Ste-Catherine Ouest.Hosting an event at Concordia? Contact Hospitality Services to find out more about your many catering options.
