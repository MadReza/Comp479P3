http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/campus-life/recreation/facilities/athletic-complex.htmlThe Athletics Complex (RA) on Concordia's Loyola campus houses our hockey arena, stadium, gymnasium, outdoor playing fields and Stinger Dome. You will also find our state-of-the art PERFORM Centre and its conditioning space, Le Centre.The Ed Meagher arena is home to Stingers ice hockey, intramurals and community programs. It meets NHL standards and is open 11 months a year.At the Concordia gym you can find a pick-up game or join in on some intramural league action: Basketball, indoor soccer, volleyball, badminton and more, we got you covered!Get on pitch and play! There are two large playing fields at Loyola: Sherbooke field houses the stadium where our Stingers play, and De Maisonneuve field beside it which, in the winter months, turns into the Stinger Dome.   514-848-2424 x3925
