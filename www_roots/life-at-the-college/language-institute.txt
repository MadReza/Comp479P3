Our English and French as a second language non-credit programs are dynamic and student-centered, designed to meet the needs of adult learners.In addition to part time study, the Language Institute offers a year-round Intensive ESL Program. Founded in 1977, this daytime program attracts international students from over 60 countries. The program integrates the four basic skills of reading, writing, listening, speaking, plus grammar and study skills, and utilizes a dynamic video and listening program in each of its 8 levels, beginners to advanced.
