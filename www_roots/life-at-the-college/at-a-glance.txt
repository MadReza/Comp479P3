http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/research/at-a-glance.htmlOur researchers are at the heart of Concordia's research enterprise. The university seeks to foster a dynamic and supportive research environment through recognizing and supporting research chairs and research centres.Read Concordia's blueprint for fostering a vibrant institutional research culture that fuels a diverse range of activities.Dozens of specialized units bring researchers together for knowledge creation and mobilization, contributing to the university's growing visibility and reputation.Concordia recognizes and values the role of research chair holders in achieving research excellence. Our research Chairs are leaders in their fields.  This section highlights the most recent recipients of prestigious prizes, awards and honors, both from within Concordia and from external organizations.Consult the list of committees chaired by the Vice-President, Research and Graduate Studies.   Participate in the newly-launched gateway to research and researchers at Concordia, the research@concordia microsite. To participate in the microsite or to modify your page, look for the MyResearch@Concordia link inside the MyConcordia portal.
