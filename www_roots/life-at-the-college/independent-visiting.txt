http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/jmsb/programs/undergraduate/non-business-students/independent-visiting.htmlIf you have received approval through CRÉPUQ to take a course at Concordia University’s John Molson School of Business you must provide proof of prerequisite completion to the Undergraduate and Student Affairs Office, located at MB 4.201 prior to registration. Please allow sufficient time for processing of prerequisite assessment. You should also bring a copy of your approval on the CRÉPUQ inter-university site.  Students may register for courses based on space availability at the start of the semester. 

For further information or assistance, please call 514-848-2424, ext. 2668 or 2721.Students visiting from out-of-province universities who wish to register for courses at JMSB should submit proof of prerequisite completion to the JMSB Undergraduate and Student Affairs Office prior to registration. Please allow sufficient time for processing of prerequisite assessment. Visiting students may register for courses based on space availability at the start of semester.For further information or assistance, please call our office at 514-848-2424, ext. 2721.Independent students may take a maximum of two JMSB courses per term, provided there is adequate space in the courses selected and that individual course prerequisites have been satisfied. Please note that since Independent Students are not enrolled in a Business program, we cannot guarantee seats in any of our Business courses.Procedures and deadlines for enrolling at Concordia University as an Independent Student are available online, or by contacting the Birks Student Service Centre on the Sir George Williams campus, located at LB-185.© Concordia UniversityConcordia UniversityVisiting studentsJohn Molson School of Business
