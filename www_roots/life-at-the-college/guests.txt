http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/graduation-convocation/guests.htmlWelcome parents and friends of our graduates to convocation! Here's information to assist you as you plan for your graduate's milestone day.The ceremonies will be held in the Théâtre Maisonneuve at Place des Arts.Graduates and their guests are asked to use the entrance located at 175 Ste-Catherine Street West.Graduates will be issued two (2) guest tickets at gown distribution. These guest tickets must be presented at the door at Théâtre Maisonneuve before the ceremony. Children under the age of five will not require tickets as long as they do not occupy a seat. Seating is general admission.After gown distribution, extra tickets may be available, to a maximum of two (2) additional tickets per graduate, starting at 10:00 a.m. until 5:00 p.m. on Friday, 4 November 2016 in the Atrium of the McConnell Building (LB Building). These tickets will be distributed on a first-come, first-served basis. Please note that graduates must present their marshalling card in order to receive these tickets.Consider preserving the moment for posterity by buying your graduate a diploma frame. They are available at the Concordia Stores, along with other convocation memorabilia.We’re so glad you’ll be joining us in Montreal. Here are some suggestions to help you make the most of your time in this exciting, world-class city. We think you’ll love it as much as we do.See our list of suggested hotels close to Concordia.
