http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/admissions/tuition-fees/permanent-code.htmlYou will pay more tuition if you do not have a permanent code.Quebec's Ministère de l’Éducation et de l’Enseignement supérieur (MEES) gives you a permanent code to identify you as a student in the Quebec education system. Universities need your permanent code to receive funds to help pay for your education.Provide Concordia with your existing permanent code – or give us the information needed to create a permanent code – and we will forward your information to the ministry.
