Concordia University and the Association of Concordia University Management and Administrative Employees (ACUMAE) are pleased to announce that on December 18, 2015, the parties reached an agreement in principle on the terms and conditions for the renewal of the ACUMAE agreement.Details of the agreement are confidential pending ratification by ACUMAE’s members and approval by Concordia’s Board of Governors.
