http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/news/main/stories/2013/05/01/concordia-and-cufa-reach-agreement.htmlConcordia University is pleased to announce that an agreement has been reached with the Concordia University Faculty Association (CUFA) on the renewal of the CUFA collective agreement.    Details of the agreement, approved on April 26 by Concordia’s Board of Governors, will be made available through the Memorandum of Agreement on the Human Resources web page. The collective agreement will be signed shortly and will take effect on the day of its signature.   “After considerable effort on the part of everyone at the bargaining table, we are pleased to have reached a successful conclusion to this round of bargaining,” says CUFA President Lucie Lequin.   “We are also pleased to reach an agreement with our full-time faculty,” says Lisa Ostiguy, interim provost and vice-president, academic affairs. “Both parties worked together to reach a common understanding on the issues, and find a solution that is fair and sustainable, particularly in light of our current financial situation.
