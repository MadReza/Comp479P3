Currently, our faculty members attain about 2.4 million dollars annually in external funding to support their research. Our laboratories are well-equipped, with ample computing facilities at the local network and university-wide, providing faculty and students with an excellent environment for conducting their research.The Applied Psychology Centre (APC) is the in-house training clinic of the department. The APC provides therapy, counselling and assessment services to the local community.The participant pool provides undergraduate students with an opportunity to learn about psychology research through participation in ongoing research projects in the department. Participation in research will broaden your knowledge of psychology and bring meaning to the material you are learning in class.
