http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/events/artsci/polisci/wssr/2017/02/09/cowantalk.html There is no better time than now, on the wake of Canada's 150th birthday, to come together and discuss the future of this country's democratic institutions. On February 9th at 5:30pm, the Honourable Senator James Cowan will join us to debate the role of the Senate in Canada and its future in Canadian democracy.
This talk is open to everyone but you must register to attend.
