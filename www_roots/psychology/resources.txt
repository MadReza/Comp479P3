http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/sexual-assault/resources.htmlIn the Sexual Assault Resource Centre, we have a resource room stocked with books and handouts on consent, sexual harassment and supporting survivors of sexual assault. We also have lists of on- and off-campus resources ready to support you.Feel free to come by during our office hours or download the resources below.Download these handouts that are also available in our resources room.  We are open to students, staff, and faculty of all genders and sexual orientations.SARC Coordinator
