On September 28, 2014, Concordia University and the Concordia University Part-time Faculty Association (CUPFA) reached an agreement in principle, on the terms for the renewal of the collective agreement.Details of the agreement in principle are confidential while the parties submit the agreement to the members of CUPFA for ratification and to Concordia’s Board of Governors for approval.
