Help shape the future of Concordia, our students or your alumni association by getting involved as a volunteer.The Board of Directors of the Concordia University Alumni Association (CUAA) is responsible for all the association's finances, policies and activities.The Board holds four evening meetings and one annual general meeting per year. Board members are invited to chair or sit on one or more committees and to participate in one strategic planning session per year.
