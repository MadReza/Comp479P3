Our graduate clinical program ranks among the best in Canada and is accredited by the Canadian Psychological Association and the Ordre des psychologues du Québec.At the beginning of graduate studies in psychology, students are admitted to the master's program, either the research option or the research and clinical training option. Both options have the same research requirements, which include completion of a master's thesis.The doctoral program provides advanced research training, culminating in the Ph.D. thesis for all students, and advanced clinical training for those following the Clinical Profile.
