In addition to the impact of our ongoing commitment to providing accessible advanced education, Concordia faculty, staff and students affect the city and the world by mobilizing around important contemporary issues, bringing their understanding, expertise and commitment with them.By identifying specific domains for intervention, developing appropriate partnerships, and then focusing our research and teaching on the targeted issues, we can amplify our impact and become a more effective agent of change with respect to the major challenges of our times. Mandate: Develop complementary city and international strategies for Concordia
Leaders: Deputy Provost Lisa Ostiguy is leading the city part of this direction and William Cheaib, Associate Vice-President, International is leading the world part of this directionRead the cross-institutional strategy for Embrace the City.
