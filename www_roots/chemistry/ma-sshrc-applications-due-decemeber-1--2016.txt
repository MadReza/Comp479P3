The deadline for Master’s students to apply for the CGS competition is December 1st
 
Applicants must:
 
a)      open an account and complete an online application on the Research Portal, located on the NSERC website
b)      select an appropriate field of research and Concordia University as one of the proposed host organizations
c)      upload all official transcripts and research proposal to the online application system
d)      create a Canadian Common CV and link it to their application; and
e)      invite two referees electronically  so that he or she may upload their reference letters
f)      click submit by December 1st, 2016
