To request a studio booking, please visit the Webcheckout PIR Calendar.  Note: Priority for studio bookings is given to those students who are registered in courses that require the studios for class lectures and or class projects.   Practice rooms are available for music students to use throughout the academic year. Most practice rooms contain pianos but some are customized for jazz practice with drum kits, guitar and bass amplifiers.The Department of Music maintains several electroacoustic studios, including a 24-track recording studio in the Oscar Peterson Concert Hall. Contact the MB Depot (see right sidebar) for more information on studios and studio use.*NOTE: Music Department studios, practice rooms, pianos and equipment are reserved strictly for Music students registered in Music courses.Non-Music students should visit the Dean of Students page for information on the university Music Room.EquipmentThe Department of Music offers students the use of equipment outside of the classroom including microphones, amps, instruments, music stands, and musical scores. Registered music students are invited to request equipment by visiting the depot in person.
