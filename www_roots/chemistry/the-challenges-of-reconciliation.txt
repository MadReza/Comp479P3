He is Chair of the Department of Indigenous Studies and professor of Business Administration at Trent University in Peterborough, Ontario. As one of Canada’s leading scholars in the field of Indigenous Studies Professor Newhouse is well known for his extensive work on Aboriginal governance and politics, Aboriginal economic development and the socioeconomic conditions of Indigenous people in urban settings.
