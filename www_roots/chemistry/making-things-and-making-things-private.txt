http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/events/main/2016/11/03/making-things-and-making-things-private.htmlIn the context of Media Literacy Week across Candaa and in collaboration with LEARN Québec join us on Thursday, November 3 from 2pm to 7pm for three super exciting events. First, a mini-maker faire with a set of four stations with specific themes and technologies on each table. Participants will rotate to explore the different tables to explore: electronic circuits, robotics and creative programming for kids, game pads makey makey, motors, robotics and LEDs. 

Second, a fabrication showcase will be setup where you can explore various projects that have been made by participants. 

Third, the official launch of "Define Privacy" an interactive documentary that explores how youth define and understand privacy in their lives.

The location, including bathrooms, are accessible for people using wheelchairs and other mobility aids.

Refreshments will be served.This event is free and open to all, but participants should register online in advance to ensure there is space. © Concordia UniversityConcordia University
