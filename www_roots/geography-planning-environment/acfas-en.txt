http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/events/conferences/acfas-en.htmlConcordia University is proud to welcome the 82nd Congress of l’Association francophone pour le savoir — Acfas in Montreal from May 12 to 16, 2014. This is a first for the university and coincides with Concordia’s 40th anniversary.Research has evolved at an unprecedented rate in the last decades. The theme chosen 2014, Research: Zones of Creativity and Convergence, reflects this evolution. Researchers, graduate students and other research professionals are invited to take part in this not-to-be-missed event.Learn more about colloquia and activities open to the public on the Acfas website
