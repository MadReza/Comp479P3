Farmers who run a CSA program deliver a variety of fresh vegetables every week to their clients, at a pre-established pick-up location.Participating in a CSA is a great way to support local organic farms and to take concrete action to help the environment!Environmental Health & Safety is home of a CSA pick-up point.
