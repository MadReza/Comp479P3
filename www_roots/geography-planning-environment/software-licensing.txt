When a new computer is purchased with Concordia funds, it is equipped with the standard software image. Additional software is available for download through the MyConcordia portal or occasionally by special request to the Service Desk.If your department has a designated technician or support team, please contact them directly. IITS supports all departments without a designated technician. For more information, visit our Get help page.
