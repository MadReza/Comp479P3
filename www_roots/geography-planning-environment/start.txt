http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/clubs/start.htmlInterested in starting a student group? Then follow these easy steps. Skip to Step 4 if you just need to register your existing student group.
Check the complete list of registered student groups to see if the group already exists, perhaps you could collaborate with them rather than starting a new group.Decide what type of student group you want to create and speak to the umbrella association your group would fit under.  Please note, each association has their own procedures for starting a club and most have deadlines for starting a club each academic year.UndergraduateGraduateOtherRequirements for consideration
Groups must re-register every year starting June 1If you want to include the word "Concordia" in your group's name, you must fill out the following form and submit it with your application: Request to use the Name ConcordiaOnce you have finished obtaining approval to become a student group from your Umbrella Association, you must register your student group. We can help.
