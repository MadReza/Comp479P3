http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/health/medical-records.htmlWe take your confidentiality very seriously. Please review our policy.We are happy to provide you (and only you) with a copy of your medical records. We do charge for this service, and ask that you pay in advance by credit or debit card:Come to Health Services and ask the receptionist. Make sure you have your student ID and a debit or credit card with you. You may ask for a complete or partial copy of your records. A nurse will assist you in the process.
Certain delays may apply depending on the size and location of your file. For mental health records, we may have to check with the psychiatrist before releasing the file.If you cannot come in person, please contact us for more details.
