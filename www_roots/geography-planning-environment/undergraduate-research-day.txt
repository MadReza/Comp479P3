http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/artsci/research/undergraduate-research-day.htmlEach year, the Faculty of Arts and Science hosts Undergraduate Research Day, an oral and poster presentation event that showcases the highest caliber research and research-creation projects of students in the social sciences, natural and health sciences, and humanities.The oral and poster presentations and multimedia works, open to all members of the Concordia University community and to the community at large, are adjudicated by Faculty researchers in all three sectors. An awards ceremony, with cash prizes in all three sectors for oral and poster presentations, and reception conclude the dynamic day-long event.© Concordia UniversityConcordia UniversityUndergraduate Research DayFaculty of Arts and Science
