http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/events/main/2016/12/08/concordia-university-annual-christmas-auction.htmlConcordia University Library Christmas Auction Committee is holding its annual Christmas Auction! 

Every year the Library Staff of Concordia University Library hold an auction to raise funds for charitable organizations in our community. This is the 27th year that we are holding our fund raising Auction to benefit:Auberge Madeleine, Can Serve Foundation, Chez Doris, CODE (Canadian Organization for Development through Education), Concordia University Centre for the Arts in Human Development (CAHD), Multi-Faith Chaplaincy and Financial Aids and Awards, Dans la Rue, the Gazette Christmas Fund, Operation Eyesight Universal, OXFAM, Sun Youth, and the Women's Centre of Montreal.

Auctionable items have included signed hockey sticks from the Montreal Canadiens to books, CDs, jams, original paintings, hand crafted jewelry, cosmetics, and gift certificates to downtown restaurants. Many one-of-a-kind items have been auctioned off! There is something for everyone!Admission: $2  
Food will be available for purchase as well.
