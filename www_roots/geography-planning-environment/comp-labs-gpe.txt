http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/it/services/comp-labs-gpe.htmlConcordia offers a wide range of computing resources, electronic, and media-related equipment to the academic community. The labs on this page are for use by members of the Geography, Planning & Environment (GPE) department. The open access lab may be used by undergraduate students in GPE courses with a computer lab component and all GPE grad students. The specialized research labs are for use by select graduate students in the GPE department. For more information about an individual lab, choose it from the documentation section below.
For a detailed explanation of who can use an specific lab, select that lab from the documentation section below.There is no cost associated with this service.
Absolute Priority is given to teaching or academic requests in the open use lab. The class schedule for each lab is posted at H-1254 and H1254-01.
The specialized research labs are available to select grad students, and the students will be given a key to access the lab.
The service availability hours only apply to the open use lab and are as follows:
Monday to Thursday:10am to 8pm, Fri-Sat-Sun: noon-5pm
 Service is greatly reduced in the summer months.
