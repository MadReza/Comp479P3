It includes fixed equipment in University spaces, portal equipment, wireless network access, and technical support available from the Service Centre locations.Equipment is for on-campus use only.Rates are determined by client and event classification.

Department faculty and staff from both academic and administrative units.Preferred clients:

Maximum borrowing time is 3 days and is subject to availability. Equipment pick ups and returns are processed during Service Centre hours.Weekend loans are to be picked up by Friday at 4:00 p.m. and are to be returned by 10:00 a.m. on the next business day.Yes, equipment rentals are available for on-campus use and are subject to availability.To modify or cancel a booking, notify the Service Desk by email at help@concordia.ca and reference the original date of the booking.                 Booking for events in Hospitality Concordia managed venues are processed through MyEvents and are assigned an event coordinator who will manage the entire booking - including live event support.For equipment loan & rental in non-Hospitality Concordia managed venues submit a request form to the Service Desk.For equipment loan & rental with a mix of Hospitality Concordia and non-Hospitality Concordia managed venues, complete the request form for the venues not serviced by your Hospitality Concordia event coordinator.To borrow a laptop from the Library, visit the Borrow a laptop service information page.To borrow a tablet (iPad) from the Library, visit the Borrow a Tablet (iPad) service information page.Only internal academic or preferred clients may choose this option.Technicians will remotely ensure that standard room controls are available to the client through the Crestron panel.No on-site support will be provided. If a technical malfunction occurs, support is provided from the Loyola Service Centre during regular business hours, on an on-call best effort basis with priority given to regular classrooms.Configuration changes to the room and training/orientation is not provided.A fee of $35.00 to set up a laptop and/or microphones will apply for non-academic events that have no on-site technician present.On-site technician billable for a minimum of 3 hours and can be requested for the duration of the event depending on the services requested.Events requiring technical services in more than two rooms require the services of two technicians.Technicians arrive 1 hour before the event for setup and are required for a minimum of 2 hours into the event.If a technical malfunction occurs following the technician’s booked period, support is provided from the Loyola Service Centre during regular business hours, on an on-call best effort basis with priority given to regular classrooms.Audio-visual equipment usage rate includes a laptop and up to 4 microphones.Valid Concordia University identification (student, faculty, or staff)
