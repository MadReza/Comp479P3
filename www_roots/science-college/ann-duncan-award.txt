http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/financial-support/scholarships-awards/internal/ann-duncan-award.htmlSubmissions are now being accepted for the 2017-2018 Ann Duncan Travel and Tuition Award At The Leonard and Bina Ellen Art Gallery.
For 2017-2018, the competition is open only to qualified undergraduate students registered in the Bachelor of Fine Arts - Studio Art program.This unique and prestigious award helps offset the cost of travel to a significant international contemporary art exhibition such as a biennial, Documenta, Manifesta or another large scale art event taking place between February 1st 2017 and January 30th 2018.The award also contributes to the cost of full-time tuition and fees at Concordia University in Fall/Winter 2017-2017.Please note that art fairs of any kind are excluded from this award.

For further information, consult the information bulletin here.Submission Deadline: Friday December 22 2016 at 5:00 p.m.
