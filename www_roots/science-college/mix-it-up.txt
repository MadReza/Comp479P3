They also pave the way for increased collaboration internally, as well as richer and more productive partnering across institutional boundaries.Fewer administrative blockages to the free flow of ideas mean a healthier, livelier, and more magnetic university.directions@concordia.ca#CUdirections© Concordia UniversityConcordia UniversityStrategic directions
