http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/artsci/english/student-life/writing-at-concordia/richler-writer-in-residence/ann-marie-macdonald.htmlAward-winning Canadian author, playwright and actor Ann-Marie MacDonald was Concordia University’s inaugural Mordecai Richler Writer in Residence, issuing digital dispatches as well as a public lecture as part of the university’s Thinking Out Loud series. Watch the video.The Richler family established Concordia’s Mordecai Richler Reading Room in 2013. The space is a recreation of the author’s writing space with his original desk, typewriter, books, papers and even his ashtray – all things that surrounded Richler as he wrote some of Canada’s most memorable works. The room is located in the J.W.
