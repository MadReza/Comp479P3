http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/financial-support/scholarships-awards/internal/queen-elizabeth-ii-silver-jubilee-endowment-fund-for-study-in-a-.htmlSubmissions are now being accepted for the Queen Elizabeth II Silver Jubilee Endowment Fund for Study in a Second Official Language Award, which is administered nationwide by Universities Canada.Concordia University is permitted to nominate 1 (one) candidate following our internal competition.The award is intended to encourage young Canadians who wish to improve their proficiency in their second official language to pursue studies, on a full-time basis, at a university which functions in the other official language and in a milieu in which that language predominates.  The Award is intended for students who have sufficient ability in their second official language to pursue their studies in that language.For details on the eligibility requirements, visit the Universities Canada website.The application form can be downloaded here.Concordia University's internal application deadline is December 1 2016 at 5:00 p.m. and candidates must deliver their completed applications and required supporting documentation to the Financial Aid and Awards Office (Guy Metro Building room GM-230).
