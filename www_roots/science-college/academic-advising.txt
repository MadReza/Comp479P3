Use this service to get help choosing courses, planning your academic progression, adding or dropping majors or minors, and making other changes to your program of study.Students who have been away from their studies for nine or more consecutive terms require advising. Students must make an appointment to meet with an academic advisor in the Undergraduate Programs Office (MB 4.201). Students whose assessment and academic standing has placed them on conditional standing must meet with an academic advisor.Students assessed as being on failed standing must apply for readmission prior to seeking advising and permission to register.JMSB Undergraduate Student Affairs Office
