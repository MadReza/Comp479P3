http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/news/main/stories/2014/05/14/acfas-gallery.htmlBeginning May 12, Concordia has been playing host to the 82nd Congress of l’Association francophone pour le savoir (Acfas), the largest multidisciplinary gathering of research and knowledge in the French-speaking world. Acfas wraps up May 16.Over the course of the week, 5,000 researchers from across the globe have been taking part in colloquia exploring subjects as diverse as art therapy and the aging process.Here's a look at what’s happening so far.
