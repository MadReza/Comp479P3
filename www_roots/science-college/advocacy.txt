http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/offices/advocacy.htmlThe Student Advocacy Office* assists you whenever you face difficult situations and helps you understand Concordia’s rules and regulations. We have been offering free, confidential, services for 23 years to both undergraduate and graduate students.*Formerly the Student Advocate Program.If you ever face charges under the Academic Code of Conduct or the Code of Rights and Responsibilities our advocates can help you prepare for the appeals process. We can also help you with Academic reevaluation proceduresFrom one-on-one advice to in-class presentations, we help students avoid Academic Code of Conduct violations by engaging in a host of preventative measures related to academic integrity. Download our brochures on issues related to academic integrity.
