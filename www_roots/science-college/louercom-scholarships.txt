With all the time and effort that students put into classes, school work and exams, it can be hard to find the means – and time to pay for not only books and tuition, but also for other necessities, such as rent, groceries and electric bills. Louer.com would like to recognize all the hard work and devotion put in by these students by awarding 4 scholarships worth a total of $2,000.For further information on eligibility requirements and deadlines, visit the Louer.com website.
