http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/jmsb/programs/graduate/mba/beyond-the-classroom/women-in-business-club.htmlThe John Molson Women in Business Club (JMWIBC) is an association open to all women - currently or previously enrolled in a graduate program at the John Molson School of Business (JMSB) - who are looking to advance their skills and career through our network of fellow students, alumna and business professionals.
JMWIBC's initiatives have been proven to contribute to the personal and professional development of our members by providing them with social and career-related networking opportunities. JMWIBC members have access to workshops, networking cocktails and events, including their most prestigious event, the Annual Women in Business Conference. Driven by passionate members with a common goal, the JMWIBC also gives back to society through its community outreach program and fundraisers. JMWIBC aims to cultivate a supportive community that promotes gender diversity in business through the advancement and empowerment of women in their personal and professional pursuits.The Women In Business Club's 2016-2017 Executive Team:
