Each volume consists of two issues, published in the summer and winter of every year.Established in 1988 and based at Concordia University, the Centre for the Study of Learning and Performance (CSLP) is a research centre consisting of over 50 principal members from 10 different research institutions.  The CSLP has led a variety of innovative research and development projects, involving hundreds of students from elementary, secondary and postsecondary educational institutions.ALERT is team of applied linguists working within the CSLP.  The ALERT project applies an innovative multi-dimensional approach to understanding and enhancing language learning.
