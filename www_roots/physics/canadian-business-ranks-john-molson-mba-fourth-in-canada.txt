http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/news/jmsb/2014/10/03/canadian-business-ranks-john-molson-mba-fourth-in-canada.htmlConcordia’s John Molson MBA has been ranked fourth in Canada in the Canadian Business Top Ten rankings.This particular ranking emphasizes “value for money”. The Canadian Business Top Ten screens for programs that provide a good classroom experience that doesn’t remove students from the workforce for an extended period, that produce a decent salary bump compared with tuition and put a recognizable name on a student’s resumé.The Canadian Business website states that the ranking relies on a mix of self-reported quantitative data from MBA programs and independent qualitative data.
