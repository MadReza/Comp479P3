http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/ccr/validator-guide.htmlValidators are staff, faculty or students who are responsible for approving or declining students’ requests for an activity to be added to their Co-Curricular Record. Validators are in charge of reviewing the CCR requests for the activities that they oversee and manage.Please note: Validators are responsible for approving or declining all pending validation requests by Tuesday May 31, 2014 at 11:59 p.m.Validators are designated on the activity request form. To become a validator, please fill out the Activity Request Form.When a student requests to add an activity to their personal record you will receive an e-mail to the address that is associated with your student account indicating there is a validation request. You can then follow the steps below to validate her/his request.Step 1: Log in to the MyConcordia PortalStep 2: Click on the “co-curricular record” tab on the left hand menuStep 3: This will bring up the co-curricular record dashboard. Click the “My Validations” button and it will bring you to a screen where you can select:“View My Validations” or “View my Record.” Select View My Validations to check what activities you have to validate. Step 4: In the My Validations section any validations requiring approval will appear under the Pending Validations tab.Make sure the student requesting to be validated was active within your group or organization. If they were, click the approve button under the “Action” heading and the activity will be added to their CCR. Select decline under the same heading if they were not. For a more detailed description of the activity you can click on the position title. Dean of Students
