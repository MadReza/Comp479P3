http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/events/offices/vpaer/aar/2016/09/15/cuaa-3rd-agm-and-special-general-meeting.htmlJoin us as we celebrate the past year of achievements of the Concordia University Alumni Association.Thursday, September 15, 2016
Reconnect with fellow alumni and hear the latest news on upcoming initiatives. As well as reflecting on the past year, we will be looking to the future by confirming the proposed 2016-2017 directors and reviewing the amended by-laws.Following the meeting, we invite you to stay for a reception to welcome our newly elected members.
