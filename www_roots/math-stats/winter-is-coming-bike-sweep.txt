The fall bike sweep is here.Cyclists may notice that some bicycles on campus have been tagged, which means they have been identified as abandoned by their owners.On November 10, Environmental Health and Safety will remove all tagged two-wheelers from the bike racks to make way for active cyclists to park.If your bike was tagged by mistake, simply remove the tag before November 10.Remember, bike racks are not meant to be long-term storage solutions.Has your bike disappeared on this exact date?
