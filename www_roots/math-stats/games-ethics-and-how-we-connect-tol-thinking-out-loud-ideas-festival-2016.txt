What are the ethics of communications technologies like video games? How do people use game spaces to supplement their daily lives?Clive Thompson, author of Smarter Than You Think: How Technology is Changing Our Minds for the Better, joins Concordia’s Mia Consalvo, a professor in the Department of Communication Studies and Canada Research Chair in Game Studies and Design, for a conversation on how games help us connect socially, the ethics of communications technologies and the impact of online activities on our off-line lives.This is the first 2016 event in Concordia's Thinking Out Loud ideas festival.Register today!© Concordia UniversityConcordia University
