One community. One university. One gift at a time.Giving to the Community CampaignMore than 8,500 Concordians — alumni, faculty, staff, parents, students and friends — contributed $2.1 million to the university’s 2015-16 Community Campaign. Your support fosters pride, sets an example and changes lives.Community Campaign programsThroughout the year, we call upon the Concordia community for support. Perhaps you've received a phone call, letter or email from us? Your willing response to any of our appeals is a contribution to the Community Campaign - a show of solidarity with Concordia.Other Community Campaign programs include:Read our brochure to learn more about how you can help advance students and shape Concordia by supporting the Community Campaign. For more information on the various giving options or for specific questions regarding your gift, please contact us at giving@concordia.ca.Brad Skog
