http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/ccr/add.htmlIn the event that the activity you participated in is not in the database you can add it by follow these steps.Step #1: Go to the MyConcordia Portal (www.myconcordia.ca) and enter your netname and password.Step #2: To access the co-curricular record, click on the “co-curricular record” link in the left-hand menu.Step #3: You will now be on the main page of the Co-Curricular Record, called the Dashboard.  Select the “Add an Activity to the CCR Database” button.Step #4: This will take you to the Guidelines section for adding an activity to the form. Review the guidelines and once you have finished, select “Click here to proceed to the activity request form.”Step #5: This will bring you to the Activity Request Form. Fill out the form making sure to provide the appropriate information in all the required fields and select the Anticipated Achievements for the position. Those with an asterisk (*) are required. Ensure an appropriate validator is selected, and click the send request button once completed.Note: A validator is responsible for approving or declining a student’s request for their participation in an activity. They should be a member from their club, or a staff/faculty member who works closely with that group and can verify the student was actively involved.
 Step #6: The activity is reviewed by a Co-Curricular Record staff member who will edits any spelling mistakes, ensure the description is in proper form (sentences, not points), and then will approve the request and send an e-mail indicating the request is approved. Dean of Students
