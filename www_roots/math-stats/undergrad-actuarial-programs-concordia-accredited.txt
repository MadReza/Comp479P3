http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/news/artsci/math-stats/2012/09/01/undergrad-actuarial-programs-concordia-accredited.htmlThe undergraduate actuarial programs at Concordia University have earned provisional accreditation at the national level from the Canadian Institute of Actuaries (CIA). This accreditation applies to courses starting on or after September 1, 2012.It gives students, who achieve certain minimum grades in their actuarial courses, the option to apply to the CIA for exemptions from the preliminary professional actuarial exams FM, MLC, MFE and C. This option consolidate students’ progress towards achieving their Fellowship designation within the CIA, and eliminate duplications between professional exams and students tests for their university studies.
