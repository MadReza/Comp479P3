http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/students/graduate/thesis/ethesis.htmlFirst and final submissions of theses to the Thesis Office are primarily electronic.To be compatible with the Library Repository (Spectrum):Students should email a copy of their thesis to Thesis.Deposit@concordia.ca along with a completed Theses Non Exclusive License form and a Doctoral/Master’s Thesis Submission form.You will receive an email with a checklist advising you of corrections, if any, required to your thesis format.Guidelines for final submission of your e-thesis.The electronic submission must be in PDF/a format (archival PDF). For information on how to deposit theses, students must log on to Spectrum using their portal name and ID. Follow instructions using the HELP button. Students will be provided with detailed instructions.A blank copy of the signature page (with examiners’ names but without signatures) must be inserted into the final electronic submission of the thesis.HelpAny questions concerning the ethesis submission can be directed to Sharon Carey at 3802 or thesis@concordia.caSchool of Graduate StudiesMary Appezzato
