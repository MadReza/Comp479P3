http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/news/main/stories/2014/09/12/buzz-bistro-is-nowallyoucaretoeat.htmlThe Buzz Bistro on the Loyola Campus has expanded to include nine food stations for one low price of $6.99 plus tax (for on-site dining only).Found on the main floor of the Student Centre (SC) Building, the bistro provides an affordable meal option to all student, staff and faculty. Available options include:While all stations are open during the peak mealtimes, off-hour stations are limited to the salad bar, soup bar, sandwich bar, pizza station, grill, beverages and desserts.The other venues on the Loyola Campus offer a variety of options, from grabbing a quick coffee to sitting down for an à la carte meal, either inside or out. They are:The Admin Café
 First floor of the Administration (AD) Building (Grab&Go)The CJ Café
 Second floor of the Communication Studies and Journalism (CJ) Building (with indoor and outdoor seating)The Science Café
 Level 0 of the Richard J. Renaud Science Complex (SP) (expanded indoor seating area)The Rez Café
 Main floor of Hingston Hall (HB) (Grab&Go)
Visit the Food Outlets page to see food offerings, hours, and locations on both campuses.
