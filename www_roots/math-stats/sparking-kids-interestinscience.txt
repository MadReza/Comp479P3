This summer, for the second year running, Concordia was involved in an exciting initiative called the Westmount Science Camp. The camp’s goal is to promote science to children ages 6 through 13.The six one-week sessions began July 7 and wrap up August 15. Led by experts from a number of science fields, the campers participated in a wide range of educational activities that included three field trips to the Loyola Campus.
