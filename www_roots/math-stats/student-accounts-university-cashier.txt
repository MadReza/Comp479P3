Working closely with students, the SAO is focused on customer service and accounts receivable. The unit liaises with other departments to ensure that students are well informed on all student account related services.As the university cashier, the SAO is responsible for managing all petty cash floats and reimbursements as well as the processing of departmental deposits for the university community. In addition, the unit also manages all Moneris merchants from inception to maintenance.Supervisor, Student Accounts: Annette Bowen
