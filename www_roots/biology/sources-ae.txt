http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/library/guides/mie/sources-ae.htmlThis list is a selection of the more important sources of information on Aerospace Engineering available at Concordia Library. It includes material held in the Webster Library (WEB) on the Sir George Williams Downtown Campus, the Vanier Library (VAN) on the Loyola Campus and/or available online. Most materials are located in the Reference area (REF). This is not a complete list. Additional resources may be found by searching CLUES, the library catalogue.These resources will help you to identify many major sources of information. You can then check in CLUES to see if the newly located items are in the library.Back to topThese resources provide definitions and background information on a topic.
