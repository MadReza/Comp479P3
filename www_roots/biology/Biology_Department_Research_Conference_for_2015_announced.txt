http://www.concordia.ca/content/shared/en/news/artsci/biology/2015/09/16/Biology_Department_Research_Conference_for_2015_announced.htmlThe Biology Department announced its plans for their annual Departmental Research Conference for 2015. At this annual weekend conference, taking place this year from September 25-27, 2015, graduate students and faculty retreat to a rustic camp in Vendée, Que., to discuss science and build collegiality away from the distractions of busy schedules and electronic devices. It features research seminars, a poster session, an afternoon of activities, and a bonfire party.
