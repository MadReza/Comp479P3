http://www.concordia.ca/content/concordia/en/alumni-friends/about-us.htmlAdvancement and Alumni Relations (AAR) is led by Bram Freedman, vice-president, Advancement and External Relations, who is also president of the Concordia University Foundation. Under his leadership, AAR friend-raises and fundraises for the university.AAR also organizes events for alumni, students, donors and friends and builds relationships with the community.AAR publishes the monthly Accent newsletter and Concordia University Magazine in winter, spring and fall for more than 195,000 alumni and friends.AAR collaborates with the Concordia University Alumni Association to offer alumni and students a rich and varied selection of benefits and services as well as social, educational and cultural events, including Homecoming, class reunions and networking opportunities.Information collected via our website or email is subject to our privacy policy.We are committed to good governance and best practices as we anticipate the needs of our Concordia partners in order to advance the university’s core academic, research and community engagement missions.To establish ourselves as leaders in our specific areas of expertise so as to safeguard and enhance Concordia’s reputation and place in Montreal, Quebec, Canada and the world.  Advancement and Alumni Relations
